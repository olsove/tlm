# The Last Megastructure: Entropy Rising

## Premise

The Megastructure is decaying.

Not passively—but actively.

Every system you restore accelerates its collapse.

---

## Core Idea

Entropy is not just a threat—it is the central mechanic:
- all actions generate instability
- systems degrade over time
- the structure resists long-term equilibrium

You must constantly balance:
- growth vs collapse
- activation vs survival

---

## Gameplay Focus

- Resource pressure and tradeoffs
- Entropy management as a core system
- Risk/reward decisions
- Controlled instability

---

## Player Experience

- Tense and strategic
- Constant decision-making under pressure
- No “perfect state”—only temporary balance

---

## Narrative Tone

- Urgent, deteriorating, high-stakes
- The structure feels like it is **failing faster the more you use it**
- You are holding something together that may not want to survive

---

## Key Question

> Are you saving the Megastructure… or accelerating its inevitable end?
