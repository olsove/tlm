# The Last Megastructure

There is no record of who built it.

No origin system.  
No construction logs.  
No surviving civilisation that claims it.

Only fragments—scattered across dead archives and corrupted signals—refer to something vast drifting beyond mapped space.

They called it many things:
- The Ark
- The Halo
- The Engine
- The Tomb

But all surviving references converge on one phrase:

> **The Last Megastructure**

---

## The Discovery

It does not orbit a star.

It does not follow known physics.

It drifts.

Slowly. Deliberately. As if guided by a purpose no longer understood.

From a distance, it appears broken:
- colossal segments fractured apart
- entire regions dark and inactive
- energy signatures fluctuating unpredictably

Yet it is not dead.

Deep within its structure, something still runs.

Systems awaken when approached.  
Signals respond.  
Doors open—sometimes.

And sometimes… they don’t.

---

## What You Control

You are not a conqueror.

You are not an emperor.

You are a **custodian of a system you do not understand**.

When you first connect to the Megastructure, only a handful of zones respond:
- a failing energy core
- a fractured control lattice
- dormant subsystems waiting for input

Everything else is:
- unstable
- inaccessible
- or actively resisting restoration

Every action you take changes the structure.

Every system you activate reveals something… and hides something else.

---

## The Truth (or What Remains of It)

The Megastructure was not built for one purpose.

It was:
- a generator
- a network
- a vessel
- a weapon
- a sanctuary

Or perhaps all of these at once.

You will not be told what it is.

You will **infer it**:
- through recovered fragments
- through system behaviour
- through anomalies that should not exist

The deeper you go, the less certain the answers become.

---

# Final Note

All three versions share the same foundation:

- A vast, ancient system
- Partial control
- Incomplete knowledge

But each answers a different question:

- **Drift Protocol** → *What is this system doing?*
- **Entropy Rising** → *Can it be sustained?*
- **Broken Orbit** → *Where— or what— is it now?*

The truth of the Megastructure is not given.

It is uncovered.

And even then…

…it may not be what you expect.
