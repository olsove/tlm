# The Last Megastructure: Broken Orbit

## Premise

The Megastructure has drifted out of reality as it was known.

It no longer exists in a stable orbit—physically or conceptually.

---

## Core Idea

The surrounding space is unstable:
- anomaly fields reshape exploration paths
- regions appear and disappear
- reality behaves inconsistently

The structure is not just damaged—it is **misaligned with its environment**.

---

## Gameplay Focus

- Exploration of shifting anomaly networks
- Dynamic map changes over time
- Event-driven encounters
- Discovery through movement and observation

---

## Player Experience

- Atmospheric and exploratory
- Uncertainty and discovery
- Less control, more adaptation

---

## Narrative Tone

- Isolated, surreal, cinematic
- The structure feels **lost**, not broken
- You are navigating something that may no longer belong to your universe

---

## Key Question

> Did the Megastructure drift… or was it displaced for a reason?
